Welcome to Simon! Game

Here's how to play:

  • Objective: Test your memory and concentration. Watch and repeat a series of colors and sounds.
  • Colors & Sounds: There are four colors: red, Green, Blue, and Yellow, each with its own sound.
  • Start Simple: It begins with one color. After each success, it adds one more color to the sequence.
  • Your Turn: Click the buttons in the exact order they lit up during the sequence.
  • Challenge Yourself: The game gets harder as you go, with longer sequences. Be careful, one mistake ends the game!
  • Winning Streak: Try to keep the streak going. The more sequences you get right in a row, the higher your score!
  • Have Fun: Simon is all about enjoying and testing your memory skills. Compete with friends to see who's the best.

Click Start Or Press Any Key to Play
